BASIC PARTS OF A COMPUTER

This is where you see everything!

Monitor

The monitor is the screen of the computer. It displays information such as text, images, and videos. It’s like the "face" of the computer, where you see everything that happens.

Press this key to type a letter!

Keyboard

The keyboard is used to type letters, numbers, and symbols. It helps you interact with the computer by entering commands and writing words.

This helps you move and click!

Mouse

The mouse is a small device that lets you move the cursor (pointer) on the screen and click on things to interact with the computer. It's like your hand’s control for the computer.

The CPU helps the computer think!

Central Processing Unit (CPU)

The CPU is the "brain" of the computer. It processes information and performs calculations. Everything you do on the computer goes through the CPU.

RAM helps the computer remember things while it's on!

Memory (RAM)

RAM (Random Access Memory) is temporary storage that helps the computer perform tasks quickly. It holds data that’s being used while the computer is on.

This is where your data is safely stored!

Hard Drive (Storage)

The hard drive is the storage device where all your files, documents, pictures, and programs are saved. It keeps data even when the computer is off.

The motherboard connects everything together!

Motherboard

The motherboard is the main circuit board that connects all the parts of the computer. It allows different components like the CPU, memory, and storage to communicate with each other.

Power is what makes the computer come alive!

Power Supply

The power supply gives the computer the energy it needs to run. Without power, the computer cannot work.

Speakers let you hear sounds from your computer!

Speakers

Speakers allow you to hear sounds from the computer, like music, videos, or game audio.

This is how your computer prints on paper!

Printer

A printer is an external device connected to the computer that prints text or images onto paper.
